Training the BS 180

June 2, 2024

A year ago i stood on a wakeboard for the first time, in the middle of august i used my vacation to really get started with my own board. This season I’ve been riding more often than last year, but my progress has been limited, I’m trying pretty hard at the backside 180.

So far I’ve managed the frontside 180 and switch FS 180. The next step is to do the backside 180 on the water surface and in combination with an ollie. In the meantime, the handover is also working ;) However, the trick still doesn’t work on the kicker.
